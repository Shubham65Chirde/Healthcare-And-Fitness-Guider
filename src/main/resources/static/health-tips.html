Core Nutrition Tips

💧 Hydrate Before Meals

Drinking 500ml of water 30 minutes before a meal can increase metabolism and help with weight loss by making you feel fuller.

🥗 The 80/20 Rule

Eat nutritious whole foods 80% of the time, and allow yourself to enjoy your favorite treats for the remaining 20%. Sustainability is key.

🍗 Prioritize Protein

Protein is the most filling macronutrient. High protein intake reduces cravings and helps maintain muscle mass during fat loss.

The Power of Consistency

It is better to work out for 15 minutes every day than to work out for 3 hours once a week. Your body responds to frequent stimuli. Focus on building habits that stick rather than chasing temporary intensity.


Remember: You don't have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great.

Workout & Recovery

😴 Sleep is Training

Muscles don't grow in the gym; they grow while you sleep. Aim for 7-9 hours of quality rest to allow hormonal balance and tissue repair.

🏋️‍♂️ Progressive Overload

To see results, you must gradually increase the weight, frequency, or number of repetitions in your strength training routine.

🚶‍♂️ NEAT Matters

Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (NEAT) like walking, gardening, or cleaning accounts for more calorie burn than a 1-hour gym session.
